Reel Meals offers optional premium subscriptions through Apple's App Store:

- **Weekly Plan:** 7-day free trial, then $1.99/week
- **Annual Plan:** $19.99/year

**All billing and payment processing is handled entirely by Apple Inc.** We do not collect, store, process, or have access to any payment card information, billing addresses, or financial data of any kind.

Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period. Your Apple ID account will be charged upon confirmation of purchase. The charge will occur within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period for renewals.

You can manage and cancel your subscriptions at any time by going to your Apple ID Account Settings on your device. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current paid period.

Free trials automatically convert to paid subscriptions unless cancelled before the trial period ends.

For refund requests, please contact Apple Support directly at https://reportaproblem.apple.com.
