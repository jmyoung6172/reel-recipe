# Privacy Policy for Reel Meals

**Last updated: June 24, 2026**

## Overview

Reel Meals ("the App") is operated by Jeffrey Young ("we", "us", or "our"). This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Data We Do Not Collect

Reel Meals does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

- We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
- We do not collect location data
- We do not collect device identifiers
- We do not use analytics or tracking tools
- We do not share any data with third parties for advertising purposes

## Data Stored on Your Device

All app data — including saved recipes, meal plans, grocery lists, and macro logs — is stored **locally on your device only** using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

## Camera and Photo Library

The App requests access to your camera and photo library solely to allow you to photograph recipes for extraction. Photos are sent directly to our processing server to extract recipe information and are not stored or retained after processing.

## Recipe Extraction

When you paste an Instagram or TikTok URL, that URL is sent to our backend server to extract recipe information from the video. No personal information is transmitted — only the URL you provide.

## AI Features

The App uses AI to generate recipe suggestions, nutrition estimates, and snack recommendations. Queries sent to our backend for these features contain only the food or recipe text you enter — no personal information.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
